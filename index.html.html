FREQUENTLY ASKED

What jobs are most in demand in 2025? +
AI Engineer, Data Scientist, Cybersecurity Analyst, Cloud Architect, UX Researcher, and Product Manager with AI focus are the most in-demand roles globally. In India, roles at the intersection of AI and domain expertise are seeing explosive salary growth.
How do I future-proof my career against AI? +
Focus on skills AI cannot replace: empathy-driven roles (UX research, counseling, teaching), oversight roles (AI auditing, ethics), creative strategy, and complex human judgment. The best move is learning to work WITH AI tools rather than competing against them.
Can I switch careers without a tech background? +
Absolutely. Many high-growth roles like Product Manager, UX Researcher, AI Content Strategist, and Growth Marketer value domain expertise over coding skills. Tools like Coursera and YouTube provide free or low-cost pathways to transition within 6-12 months.
What are the best free courses to upskill in 2025? +
Google career certificates on Coursera (UX Design, Data Analytics, Cybersecurity, Project Management) are industry-recognized and affordable. YouTube channels like Andrej Karpathy (AI), Kevin Powell (web dev), and Nielsen Norman Group (UX) are world-class and completely free.
Which careers have highest salaries in India in 2025? +
AI/ML Engineer (18-40 LPA), Cloud Architect (20-45 LPA), Data Scientist (12-30 LPA), Product Manager (15-35 LPA), and Cybersecurity specialist (10-25 LPA) are among the highest paid tech roles in India currently.
