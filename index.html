It's a small commitment that keeps slots for people who are serious — it filters out no-shows so I can give you my full attention. It's fully credited toward any paid engagement you book afterward.

Over video call. You book, complete a short pre-session form, we meet, and you leave with clear next steps. Async support over WhatsApp/email is included in ongoing packages.
